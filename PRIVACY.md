# Privacy Policy for Airtime

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

Airtime is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data relative to the Airtime Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
Airtime collects the domains of the websites you visit and the duration of your stay. This data is used solely to provide you with productivity analytics and insights within the extension's dashboard.

## 2. Data Storage
**All data is stored locally on your device.** Airtime uses the `chrome.storage.local` API. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, cloud storage, or third-party services.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Your focus data never leaves your computer.

## 4. User Control
Users have full control over their data. You can view, manage, and delete your history at any time through the Airtime dashboard. Uninstalling the extension will also remove all locally stored data.

## 5. Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. By keeping all data on your local machine, we eliminate the risks associated with remote data storage.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
